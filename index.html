Our Journey

We are a family run business started in 1992 by Shri. Mahantesh Iranna Dhang, continuous hard work and dedication to quality and passion for tea made us to win millions of hearts by providing rich quality tea in attractive packing and competitive prices to our customers. Today we are with over three decades of experience in procurement, blending and processing of different grade teas, Daneshwari Tea Group has firmly entrenched itself in the hearts of millions of tea consumers in Karnataka and Maharastra states in India.
